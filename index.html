About The Hackathon

HackAttack is a high school hackathon held on August 30, 2020 from 8AM to 8PM, targeted towards students in the Sacramento area. It strives to be an all-inclusive event that engages students and drives them to express their creativity by utilizing STEM skillsets, and challenges them to use code to tackle a real-world problems. We hope to spread awareness to the fact that anyone can use technology as a tool for their near future.

FAQs

Who can attend?

Any student can attend Hack Attack!

What if I don't have a team?

Hackathons are all about meeting new people and sharing common intrests. We will help you find a team to work with.

What do I do if I have no prior experience?

You should attend! We highly encourage those new to coding to learn alongside mentors and participate in the workshops.

How large can teams be?

Teams are limited to 4 participants.

What do I need for Hack Attack?

You need a stable internet connection, a computer, a mic (recommended), and a discord account

Other Questions?

If you have any other questions or concerns, feel free to contact HackAttack at team@hackattack.tech
